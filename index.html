Our Formula (10 Key Points)

  1. Discovery: Mood, locations, outfits, and deliverables aligned before the shoot.
  2. Golden Hour First: Schedule for best light; buffer for weather and travel.
  3. Shot List: Must-have frames + room for spontaneity.
  4. Gear Ready: Dual cards, backups, prime & zoom combo, and light modifiers.
  5. Direction: Natural prompts for relaxed, candid expressions.
  6. Safety & Permits: Location rules, permits, and respect for nature.
  7. Editing: Clean, consistent grading; calibrated color workflow.
  8. Delivery: Private gallery links, print-release on request.
  9. Revisions: Shortlist swaps or light tweaks within scope.
  10. Archiving: Redundant backups for peace of mind.
